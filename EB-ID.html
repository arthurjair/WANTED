Goodnews asked us to make a media campaign for their new website Explore Breda and we could choose which target audience to campaign to.

What I did

For this project, I was excited to create a video for the media campaign and design posters. Inspired by the film "Trainspotting" and its powerful "Choose life" intro, a group member and I developed a similar concept: - "Choose Breda" - "Choose strolling in the park with your significant other," showcasing the park - "Choose..." - "Choose..." - "Choose..." - "Choose Breda… Explorebreda.nl" I saw great possibilities with this approach and was eager to bring it to life.

Wes anderson example

How I did it

I developed a concept that my group liked, so I began planning the filming locations to showcase Breda. I wrote a script but needed music. We couldn't use "Born Slippy" by Underworld due to copyright and recognizability. Finding a suitable replacement was challenging. We filmed in Breda, but the gloomy weather ruined the footage. The next day, a group member and I decided to pivot from the “choose life” idea. We brainstormed and decided to emulate Wes Anderson’s style, known for his unique color palettes and symmetry, for our project.

Our Video

Going back to Breda on a sunny day, we had new motivation. We filmed every scene we needed for our new concept. And were pleased with the results!

Full video

FINAL DESIGN

The final design concept was primarily my group member's idea, but I contributed by modernizing it with animations and helping create the About Me page.
We took a risk by changing the entire design to a more modern and bold look. On presentation day, we were excited but apprehensive about her reaction. Ultimately, it was a success; she chose our design because it resonated with her and stood out from the other groups. Sometimes, taking a risk pays off.

How it helped

This project has greatly influenced teachers' design expectations of me. Initially labeled as simple and safe, I was scared. Now, I aim to make each design more modern and daring to ensure my work stands out.
