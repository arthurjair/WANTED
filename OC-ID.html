What I did

At the beginning of the semester, I participated in a mini project where I worked in a group to create a team game similar to SpaceTeam. We developed a paper prototype to allow players to interact with the game, ensuring that teamwork was a key component of the experience.

How I did it

We began with brainstorming sessions to explore game concepts requiring teamwork. Inspired by "Overcooked," we introduced a twist with an octopus character, where each tentacle represented a player. In our game, players collaborated under limited resources and time to complete tasks efficiently. Failure to do so would result in undercooked burgers, adding urgency and challenge to the gameplay.

How it helped

This mini assignment was my first experience collaborating in a group where everyone contributed ideas, emphasizing the importance of listening to each other. We carefully considered every aspect of the game, from tasks and utensils to creating paper items for gameplay. This experience taught me the significance of attention to detail and how small elements can enhance the overall project.
