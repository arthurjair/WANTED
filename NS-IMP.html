My first project for semester 2 was to help Dutch painter Nathalie Silverentand broaden her audience and update her presence to look more professional.

What I did

Despite the short timeframe, the Nathalie Silverentand project aimed to give Nathalie a complete experience of how her new website would feel.

How I did it

I knew animation would modernize her website, so my group and I used Figma prototype to make it interactive. We added animations and made every button functional, presenting a fully interactive website instead of static images.

How it helped

This project has greatly influenced teachers' design expectations of me. Initially labeled as simple and safe, I was scared. Now, I aim to make each design more modern and daring to ensure my work stands out.
