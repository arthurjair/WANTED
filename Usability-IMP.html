"You orient in the relevant tech, media and design landscape and create interactive media products that you have tested with users and stakeholders."

What I did

I took on the task of overhauling Martijn Trading's outdated website, which serves as a distributor of goods and healthcare products in Aruba.

Martijn Before

How I did it

To enhance the website, I gathered user feedback through interviews and a user test plan, identifying key areas for improvement. I simplified the design, reduced text, and infused Martijn Trading's corporate colors for a fresher look. Adding a footer improved navigation and access to essential contact information.

After

How it helped

By implementing these changes, the website became more visually appealing and easier to navigate. Users found it simpler to understand the company's offerings and locate contact details, leading to improved usability and overall user satisfaction.
